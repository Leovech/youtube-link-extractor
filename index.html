YouTube Link Extractor

Paste a playlist or channel URL — get every video link.

What to paste

  • Playlist: open the playlist on YouTube and copy the URL from the address bar.
    Example: https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLxxxxxxxx
    A watch URL with &list= works too.
  • Channel: open the channel page and copy the URL.
    Supported formats: youtube.com/@handle, youtube.com/channel/UCxxxxx, youtube.com/user/name.
    Tip: the @handle URL is the most reliable.
